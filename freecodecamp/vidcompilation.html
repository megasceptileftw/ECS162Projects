How to start playing Weiss Schwarz

Weiss Schwarz is a magical trading card game that allows you to play with your favorite anime waifus in a battle to the death

Official Weiss Tutorial

Here is the official Bushiroad Weiss tutorial YouTube video. It is amazing for new players who do not understand the rules.

Tressa's Collectibles

Tressa's Collectibles is a Weiss Schwarz YouTube Channel that does gameplay videos that are very informative on how to play the game. If you want to improve your gameplay, you should watch them.

Japanese Weiss Schwarz

Weiss Schwarz is a Japanese TCG, so the Japanese content creators are the best. Specfically, GAHAHA Channel (ガハハチャンネル) is the most popular Japanese Weiss channel that covers fun games and tournament games. You should definitely check them out if you don't mind watching videos in Japanese.
