How to play Weiss Schwarz

Weiss Schwarz is a multimedia Trading Card Game made by Bushiroad that integrates beloved anime and game properties in a collectible form.

What do you need?

  • Playmat
  • Deck
  • Friends
  • Brain

Instructions

  1. Set up your playmat and deck
  2. Shuffle to randomize the deck
  3. Draw 5 cards
  4. Discard as many cards as you want into the waiting room and draw that many cards
